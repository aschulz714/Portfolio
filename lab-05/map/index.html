Breweries of North America and Hawaii

Authored by: Alex Schulz

The North America and Hawaii Breweries map shows brewery density using 50km hexagons. The areas of North America with the most breweries per 50km hexagon follow the pattern of major cities.The top three areas with the highest brewery density in North America are Denver, San Diego, and Seattle.

Check out the list of North America brewery locations with a link to the GeoJSON

To the right, is a closer look at the area with the third-most breweries: Seattle. Within the Seattle-area, the Ballard neighborhood offers the most breweries per 2km inner diameter hexagon. Ballard is home to the Lagunitas Brewery; one of my personal favorites.
